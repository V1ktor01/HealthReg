Help

  • How to start:

    • When you start the program you need to press "Register" to make an account!


  • Regsiter a day:

    • Press the button saying "Register your day!"

    Press smiley and press on the buttons that is what you have done today...

    Press on the activites you have done today. Press one time to make it green (this means that when you did this today it went great!)

    Press one more time on the green button to make it red (this means that it did not went so well today..)

    Write down what you will try do better tomorrow!


  • Show your stats:

    • Press on the stats button to enter your stats!


  • Delete logs:

    • Press on the settings button

    Press on the logs you want to delete and press on the delete button!
